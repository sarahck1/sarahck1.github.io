All About Hello Kitty!

Learn more about the iconic Hello Kitty!

Appearance and Personality

Hello Kitty, also known as Kitty White, is a white cat typically seen wearing her signature red bow and blue overalls. Kitty does not have a mouth. This is because the creators want people to be able to project their own feelings onto kitty. She is "five apples" tall and weighs "three apples." She enjoys baking, collecting cute things, and in school she enjoys music, art, and english class. However, most of all, she loves her friends and her sister Mimmy.

Friends

Hello Kitty has many friends. Some of her closests friends are: My Melody (her best friend), Cathy, Chip, Fifi, Jody, Judy, Moley, Rorry, Thomas, Pochacco, Pekkle, Keroppi, Keroleen. She loves spending time with her friends and is often portrayed in media alongside her friends. Many marketing campaigns are labeled "Hello Kitty and Friends", since she is most often seen with her friends.

Origin

Hello Kitty was first debuted in Japan in 1974. She was originally intended to be part of gift-giving merchandise. She first appeared on a coin purse, which sold extremely well. She appeared in the U.S. in 1976. Since then, her popularity has grown and she is still very prevalent today around the world.
